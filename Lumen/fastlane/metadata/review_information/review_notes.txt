Lumen is a daily affirmation app that works 100% offline. No account or login is required.

TESTING NOTES:
- The app is fully functional without any network connection.
- No sign-in is needed — the app starts immediately after onboarding.
- Free tier includes 8 affirmation categories, procedural backgrounds, favorites, and text-to-speech.
- Pro features (all categories, card editor, custom affirmations) require an active subscription via StoreKit 2 / RevenueCat.
- The "Grief" and "Chronic Illness" categories are marked as sensitive and require explicit opt-in by the user in Settings.
- Crisis resources (helpline links) are accessible from the Settings screen and from sensitive category views.
- Notifications permission is requested during onboarding for daily reminders but is entirely optional.
- The app supports English and Spanish localizations.
- Widget functionality can be tested by adding Lumen widgets to the home screen (small, medium, large sizes available).
