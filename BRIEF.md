# Lumen — Creative Brief

## One-liner
**Lumen** is a premium iOS affirmation app that delivers kind, personalized daily affirmations over stunning procedural and AI-generated backgrounds — designed to feel like a beautiful ritual, not a self-help chore.

## Tagline
*Daily affirmations that feel kind — not forced.*

## What it does
Lumen curates a daily feed of affirmations matched to the user's chosen categories (self-love, confidence, calm, gratitude, resilience, abundance, and more), tone preference (gentle, warm, direct, bold, spiritual), and content filters. Each affirmation is displayed as a full-screen card over a rich, immersive background — either procedurally generated (aurora, cosmos, watercolor, stained glass, waves, prism, topography, and more) or AI-generated on-device using Stable Diffusion.

Users can customize any card's typography, background style, and color palette; create their own affirmations with ML-powered suggestions; favorite and organize affirmations; listen via text-to-speech; share as beautiful images; and explore a growing library of 2,000+ affirmations across 15+ categories.

## Visual identity
- **Dark, immersive UI** — deep ambient blacks (#0A0E1A → #131B2E), glassmorphic cards, no light mode
- **Rich backgrounds** — 12 procedural styles × 14 color palettes × 5 moods = 840+ unique combinations
- **Typography-forward** — 10 curated fonts (Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, Caveat, Dancing Script, Abril Fatface, Josefin Sans, Zilla Slab, Righteous, SF Rounded, SF Pro Heavy), predominantly serif, weighted 70% toward New York-style editorial serif
- **Color language** — warm flames, ocean breeze, golden hour, deep forest, cherry, sakura, electric, frozen dreams, moonlight, night fade
- **Accent colors** — gentle teal (#7FBBCA), warm coral (#E8A87C), soft purple (#A688B5), mint green (#7EC8A0)
- **Glass and light** — semi-transparent card surfaces, ambient glow orbs, light leaks, subtle film grain

## Mood and tone
- Calm, kind, warm — like a thoughtful friend, not a motivational poster
- Sophisticated and quiet — premium feel without being cold
- Inclusive — categories for grief, body image, LGBTQ+ identity, chronic illness (sensitive, opt-in)
- Never preachy, never toxic-positive — "gentle mode" filters out high-intensity or absolute language

## Target audience
- Adults (25–45) who want a daily mindfulness ritual
- People who've tried affirmation apps but found them cheesy or generic
- Design-conscious users who care about aesthetics
- Anyone going through a tough time who wants something that feels supportive, not performative

## Key screens (for video/mockup reference)
1. **Onboarding** — animated gradient welcome → category picker → tone selector → reminder setup
2. **Feed (For You)** — full-screen swipeable affirmation cards with ambient backgrounds, crossfade transitions
3. **Explore** — grid of glassmorphic category cards, premium badge on locked categories
4. **Card Editor** — customize background style, palette, font, seed; live preview
5. **Theme Generator** — procedural + AI mode toggle, style/palette/mood pickers, generate button
6. **Favorites** — split view: "My Affirmations" (user-created) + "Favorites" (curated), slideshow mode
7. **Crisis View** — warm, supportive resource page with crisis helpline links
8. **Settings** — dark glass panels, subscription management, content filters

## Tech stack (for context, not for creative use)
- SwiftUI + SwiftData, iOS 26+, Swift 6.2
- On-device CoreML Stable Diffusion for AI backgrounds
- RevenueCat for subscriptions (Monthly $3.99, Yearly $19.99, Lifetime $49.99)
- Fully offline-capable, no server dependency

## Pro (premium) features
- Access to all 15+ categories (free users get ~8)
- AI background generation
- Custom affirmation creation with ML suggestions
- iCloud sync (coming soon)
