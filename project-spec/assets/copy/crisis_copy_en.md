# Crisis / Get help now (en-GB)

## Headline
If you feel unsafe or in danger, please get help now.

## Body (short)
- If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call your local emergency number right now.
- If you can, reach out to someone you trust and stay with them.
- If you’re thinking about self-harm, you deserve support — you don’t have to handle it alone.

## Buttons
- Call emergency number (user selects country/region; do not auto-detect via location in MVP)
- Find local support (opens a list or external link)
- I’m not in crisis (dismiss)

## Notes
- Keep this screen always accessible from Settings and onboarding.
- Do not require sign-in.
