Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to use Lumen?

No. Lumen works immediately — no sign-up, no login, no email required.

Does Lumen work offline?

Yes. Lumen is 100% offline. All affirmations, backgrounds, and features work without an internet connection.

How do I restore my Pro subscription?

Open Lumen → Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases. Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID and will restore automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Lumen → Cancel. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.

What is Gentle Mode?

Gentle Mode filters out high-intensity affirmations and absolute language. It's designed for hard days when you need softer, more grounding words. You can toggle it in Settings.

What are sensitive categories?

Categories like Grief and Chronic Illness are marked as sensitive and are hidden by default. You can opt in to them in Settings → Content Preferences. They're written with extra care and never include dismissive language.

Can I create my own affirmations?

Yes (Pro feature). Go to Favorites → My Affirmations → Create. Lumen provides ML-powered suggestions as you type.

How do widgets work?

Long-press your home screen → tap the + button → search for Lumen. Choose from small, medium, or large widgets. They display a fresh affirmation with a gradient background.

Is my data private?

Completely. Lumen collects no data, uses no analytics, and stores everything locally on your device. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

What iOS version do I need?

Lumen requires iOS 18 or later.
