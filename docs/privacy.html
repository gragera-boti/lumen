Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 21, 2026

Lumen ("we", "our", or "the app") is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your quiet moments belong to you — and we built Lumen to reflect that.

Data We Collect

None. Lumen does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app works 100% offline and does not require an account, login, or internet connection to function.

What Stays on Your Device

  • Your affirmation preferences, favorites, and history
  • Custom affirmations you create
  • Notification settings and schedules
  • Category and tone preferences
  • Theme and typography choices

All of this data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. We never have access to it.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync (optional), your data is synced through Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. This data is encrypted and managed by Apple under their privacy policy. We do not operate any servers and have no access to your iCloud data.

Subscriptions

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID — no personal information, email, or name is shared. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details.

Analytics

Lumen does not include any analytics SDKs, trackers, or third-party measurement tools. We do not track your behavior, screen views, or usage patterns.

Notifications

If you opt in, Lumen sends local notifications as daily affirmation reminders. These are scheduled entirely on-device and do not involve any server communication.

Crisis Resources

Lumen includes links to external crisis helplines (such as the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline). Tapping these links opens your phone's dialer or browser. We do not track whether you access these resources.

Children's Privacy

Lumen is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the revised version here with an updated effective date. Since we collect no data, changes are unlikely to affect you.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at lumen@gragera.dev.
